About

Revolant brings the soundtrack of the 2000s scene era back to life with loud guitars, huge choruses, and the songs everyone still knows by heart.

From Fall Out Boy and Paramore to A Day to Remember and My Chemical Romance, Revolant delivers high-energy sets designed to keep crowds engaged from the first chord to the final singalong.

Based in the Washington, DC area, the band pairs nostalgic song selection with professional production, and an organized, easy-to-work-with approach for venues and bookers.
